User Guidelines for Our MemeStore Social Media Platform

Welcome to our meme-sharing community! These guidelines help keep the platform fun, safe, and respectful for everyone. By using the app, you agree to follow them. Violations may result in content removal or account suspension.

1. Content Rules

  • Post original or properly credited memes. No stealing content without permission.
  • Avoid harmful content: No hate speech, violence, harassment, or discrimination based on race, gender, religion, etc.
  • Keep it PG-13: No explicit nudity, gore, or adult themes. Memes should be fun and light-hearted.
  • No spam: Don't flood the feed with repetitive posts or ads.

2. Community Interactions

  • Be respectful in comments and replies. Constructive humor is encouraged, but no bullying.
  • Report bad behavior: Use the report button for inappropriate content or users.
  • No doxxing or sharing personal info without consent.
  • Engage positively: Likes, shares, and remixes should promote creativity.

3. Account and Privacy

  • One account per person: No fake profiles or bots.
  • Protect your privacy: Don't share sensitive info in memes or profiles.
  • We respect your data: Review our privacy policy for details on how we handle information.

4. Copyright and Ownership

  • Your memes are yours, but by posting, you grant us a license to display them.
  • Respect copyrights: Don't use protected images or templates without rights.
  • If you see infringement, report it.
